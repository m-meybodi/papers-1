3. Open synchronous cellular learning automata
Beigy, H. (Dept. of Comput. Eng., Amirkabir Univ. of Technol., Tehran, Iran); Meybodi, M.R. Source: The 8th World Multi-Conference on Systemics, Cybernetics and Informatics, 2004, pt. 16, 9-14 Vol. 16
Conference: The 8th World Multi-Conference on Systemics, Cybernetics and Informatics, 18-21 July 2004 , Orlando, FL, USA
Publisher: IIIS, Orlando, FL, USA

Abstract: In this paper, we introduce open cellular learning automata and then study its convergence behavior. It is shown that for a class of rules called commutative rules, the open cellular learning automata in stationary external environments converge to a stable and compatible configuration. The numerical result also confirms the theory (6 refs.)
